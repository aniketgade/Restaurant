Restaurant Description

Crazy Burger serves American roadhouse food prepared from scratch using fresh ingredients. The spacious, airy interior features modern furniture and old-school charm. Stepping into the saloon-like rooms, guests can sense more than 100 years of hospitality. The proprietors strive to maintain that warm tradition and leave everyone feeling relaxed, well fed and eager to return.
